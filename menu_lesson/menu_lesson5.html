Grammar

1. Kami, Kita, Kalian dan Mereka

Kami, Kita ,Kalian and Mereka are used when the speaker,the listner,or the one being talked about are plural in number. for example :

Jane : Saya Jane.
Billy : Saya Billy.
Jane and Billy : Kami dari Australia.

In Indonesia,kami is used when the listener(s) are exclusive within the conversation being conducted.

Billy : Jane , hari ini kita makan di mana ?
Jane : Kita makan di restoran saja.

kita,is used when the speaker and the listener are inclusive within the conversation being conducted.

Nyoman : Selamat pagi Billy,Jane . Kalian mau pergi kemana?.
Jane and Billy : Kami mau pergi ke taman mini .

Kalian in Indonesian is used to address the listener are inclusive then they are plural in number.

Andi : Teman saya bernama Billy dan Jane. sekarang mereka sedang memesan makanan . Billy memesan gado-gado dan Jane memesan gude. mereka suka makanan indonesia.

In indonesia,mereka is used when the ones being talked are more than one.

Further,kami, kita, kalian and mereka can be used to state possesiveness.
For example :

(1) Saya dan Billy memesan makanan. Pelayan sedang mencatat pesanan kami .
I and Billy are ordering the food,a waiter is writing our order
(2) Makanan mereka terasa pedas.
Their food taste hot.
(3) Negara kita sangat indah.
Our country is very beautiful.
(4) Restoran Kalian sangat ramai.
your restaurant is very crowded.

2. Negasi dengan bukan dan tidak

In Indonesia, the statement can be negated by bukan or tidak. Bukan is used to negate things or people , while tidak is used to negate acting words or adjectives(qualifying words).
For example :

(1) Saya Billy. → saya bukan Billy.
i am Billy. → i am not Billy.
(2) Dia Jane. → Dia bukan Jane.
She is Jane. → She is not Jane.
(3) Anda Pelukis. → Anda bukan pelukis.
You are a painter. → You are not a painter.
(4) Ini gado-gado. → ini bukan gado-gado.
this is gado-gado. → this is not gado-gado.
(5) itu Hotel Merdeka. → Itu bukan Hotel Merdeka.
That is Hotel Merdeka .→ That is not Hotel Merdeka.

(6) Billy suka es cendol .→ Billy tidak suka es cendol.
Billy likes es cendol. → Billy does not like es cendol.
(7) Jane suka berolahraga ,→ Jane tidak suka berolahraga.
Jane likes sport. → Jane does not like sport.
(8) mereka berwisata. → mereka tidak berwisata.
They make a tour. → They do not make a tour.
(9) Indonesia itu luas → Indonesia itu tidak luas.
Indonesia is spacious. → Indonesia is not spacious.
(10) Jeni Cantik. → Jeni tidak cantik.
Jane is beautiful. → Jeni is not beautiful.

Besides bukan dan tidak, negation can be also formed by using tanpa which mean "without".
For example :

(1) Billy minum es teh tanpa gula.
Billy drank iced tea without sugar.
(2) penerbangan ini tanpa asap rokok.
This flight is a no smooking flight.
